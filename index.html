Our Story

We met our junior year of high school in the super romantic setting of the National Honors Induction ceremony in the school gym—all it took was for Kayleigh (after seeing him across the hallway) to ask Zach to sit next to her. After that, we became pretty inseparable. Our first date was a double date to see "The Dark Knight" in theaters and on October 16, 2008, we became boyfriend and girlfriend. From then on, we continued to grow through life together.

We graduated from East Carolina University together—Zach with a Finance degree, Kayleigh with a Graphic Design/Art History degree—and, after, moved in together in Uptown Charlotte with our energy-ball of a dog, Sadie.

We've already made a million memories... and, now, 8 years to the day of becoming boyfriend and girlfriend, we will be saying "I do" and beginning our adventure to make a million more!
